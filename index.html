About Our Company

Dedicated to Excellence in Technical Services.

Established in Dubai, Sumsuddin Technical Services L.L.C is a premier maintenance and technical contracting company. We specialize in providing a wide array of services ranging from essential plumbing and electrical works to complete building cleaning and aesthetic finishing.

Under the leadership of Mr. Mohammad Sumsuddin Abdul Satter, our team is committed to delivering high-quality, reliable, and timely services to our clients across the UAE. We operate under strict compliance with the Department of Economy and Tourism.
